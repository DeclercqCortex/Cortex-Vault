The Gompertz growth model is **a sigmoid (S-shaped) <mark class="mark-green">mathematical function used to model</mark> processes where growth is slowest at the start and end, approaching an upper asymptote (maximum capacity) more gradually than a logistic curve**. Originally for human mortality, it is now widely used for population, tumor, and bacterial growth.
